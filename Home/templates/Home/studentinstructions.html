Student Instructions


Update Profile


1. After logging in, you are redirected to “Submit Request Page”.

2. Firstly, Update your Profile.

  • To update the profile, click on “profile” from the bar on top left corner (Mobile View).

  • Click on the yellow bar which says “Update Profile”.

  • Fill out the details correctly.

  • Please mention a valid E-mail address, It’s used to send you a password reset link in case you forget password.

  • Mention valid mobile numbers, without a valid mobile number your request will be rejected.

  • Choose a profile picture of around 1mb where your face is visible clearly, it will be verified by security.

  • Click on “Submit” button.

  • Your Profile gets updated!

Instructions to Submit New Request:

  • Select the type you required. Select “Home-Out” for home-out request and “Day-Out” for a day-out request.

  • Set your Room number.

  • Select Out-Date.

  • Select Time. In Time slot you will be given 5 choices, Select according to your need and change if necessary.

  • Same with “In-Date & Time”.

  • Your Out-Date, Time and In-Date, Time should be given correctly. If not, your request will not be submitted.

  • Give your reasons.

  • Click on “Submit” button.

  • If you fill the form correctly, your request gets submitted and you will get a message “Request Submitted” on your Screen.

Pending Requests:

  • You can view requests that are not yet reviewed by the respective caretaker under “Pending Requests” Column.

  • You can delete your request by clicking on “Delete” button.

Approved Requests:

  • Requests accepted by Care Taker will be shown under “Approved Requests”.

  • You will be given a QR Code.

  • You can cancel your approved requests by Removing it.

Active Requests:

  • Once your QR Code gets scanned by security, your Request will be shown in Active Request.

  • You can Still view Your QR Code. This is used for getting into hostel again.

Past Records:

  • Once your QR Code is scanned by security for the second time during the “in” , Your QR Code will get expired and your records will be shown in “Past Records”.

  • All your past records will be shown here.

Rejected Requests:

  • If your request gets rejected by any cause or reason, then you can view that in “Rejected Requests” column.

All You Need to Know about QR Code


  • Once your request gets approved, You will be given QR Code.

  • You need to show QR Code to the Security and get it scanned. (You will find this QR Code in Approved Request Column)

  • And then you will be allowed to leave the campus.

  • When you return, once again you need to get the QR Code scanned near the security. (You will find this QR Code in Active Request Column)

  • And then you will be allowed to enter the campus.

  • QR Code will automatically get deleted.
