Caretaker Instructions

  • After Logging In you are redirected to the “Submit Request” page.

  • Here, you can see all the requests sent by students under your Hostel.

  • Click on “Request Review” button to see all the information of a student who sent the request. (like Parent mobile number, Address, Out date, In date etc.).

  • There you can verify the student details.

  • After conforming with the parent, you can choose your option respectively. (Either Approve or Reject).

  • You can list out students using Roll Number and year in pending request column.

  • Once you accept, the student gets a QR Code and his info will be seen in the column - “Approved Requests”.

  • Information of Students who are out of campus under the Type – “Day-Out” will be shown in separate column.

  • All the students data who went out of campus is listed under “All records” Column. You can get records of a particular day by entering the date.

  • You can also get data of particular branch or year by entering the year in given search bar.

  • You can view student status in “Student Status” column.

  • You can update your profile in Profile Column, Profile picture should of size less than 1mb.
